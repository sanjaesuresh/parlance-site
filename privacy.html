Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 5, 2026
App: Parlance — AI Speech Coach
Questions? parlance.app@gmail.com

What We Collect

On your device (never leaves unless noted below)
  • Your display name, username, and avatar — stored locally in SwiftData
  • Your session history — scores, transcripts, XP, achievements, streaks
  • App preferences — theme, notification settings, sound effects
Transmitted to our servers
  • Your speech transcript and session metrics (speaking rate, pitch, filler count) are sent to our AI provider to generate coaching feedback. This happens once per completed session.
Transmitted for Pro subscribers only
  • Your audio recording is briefly uploaded to our AI emotion analysis provider to detect vocal tone (confidence, nervousness, enthusiasm). The audio is analyzed immediately and is not stored.

How We Use It

  • To generate personalized AI coaching feedback after each session
  • To track your progress, streaks, and achievements locally
  • To display your stats in the weekly league and on your profile
  • We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties beyond the AI processing services described above

AI Processing

Parlance uses Claude AI (Anthropic) to generate session feedback. For Pro subscribers, we also use Hume AI for vocal emotion analysis. Both services process your data solely to generate your coaching results and do not store or use your data for their own model training under our agreements with them.

All AI-generated scores and coaching feedback are for practice purposes only. They are not professional coaching, psychological assessment, or any form of certified evaluation.

Data Storage & Security

  • Session data, transcripts, and your profile are stored on your device only using Apple's SwiftData framework
  • Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Audio recordings are deleted from your device immediately after processing

Your Rights

  • Delete your data: Go to Profile → Settings → Reset All Data to permanently delete everything stored on your device
  • Access: All your data is on your device — you can view it within the app at any time
  • Portability: Your data stays on your device. We do not hold a copy to export
