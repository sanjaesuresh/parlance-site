Get help with Parlance, AI Speech Coach

After each session, your speech transcript and metrics (speaking rate, pitch variance, filler word count, and pacing) are sent to Google Gemini. Gemini analyzes your response against the prompt and coaching tips, then returns a score and written feedback. Pro subscribers also receive a vocal tone score powered by Hume AI, which analyzes confidence, nervousness, and enthusiasm in your voice. All scores are for practice purposes only.
Pro unlocks four things: all 11 practice modes (the free tier includes Job Interview, Daily Convo, Impromptu, Explain a Topic, and Networking; Pro adds Pitch, Keynote, Debate, Storytelling, Negotiation, and Real Life), difficulty levels 7–10 (Advanced and Expert), vocal emotion analysis via Hume AI (your audio is analyzed for tone dimensions like confidence, nervousness, and enthusiasm), and unlimited daily sessions. Real Life mode is Pro-only and lets you describe an actual conversation you're about to have. The AI then rewrites it as a coaching prompt with focus areas tailored to your scenario. Free users can complete up to 2 sessions per day and receive full text-based AI coaching. Pro is $4.99/month.
You can browse practice prompts and review past sessions offline. Generating AI feedback requires an internet connection: your transcript is sent to our AI providers once per completed session. If you're offline when a session ends, the app will retry when connectivity is restored.
Parlance records your voice during practice sessions to generate a transcript and measure speech metrics (rate, pauses, filler words). Transcription is handled by Apple Speech Recognition, which may run entirely on-device or be processed by Apple's servers depending on your device and language. Pro subscribers' audio is briefly sent to Hume AI for vocal tone analysis, then discarded. It is not stored on any server.
